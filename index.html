Who We Are

The Layton City Zoo has been focused on saving animals that have been injured or need rehabilitation. Since 2004, we have preserved the lives of many animals, who wouldn't have been able to survive otherwise. We are also very concerned with the preservation of endangered species, so whenever the opportunity arises to take care of one, we take them under our wings and make sure they live long, happy lives.

Here at the Layton Zoo we ensure that:

  • Animal dietary needs are met and exceeded
  • Animal habitats are cleaned and sanitized daily
  • Vet Checkups occur every 2 weeks

We are happy to answer any questions that you have for us!
